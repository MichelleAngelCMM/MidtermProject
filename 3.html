ISPSC ranks 3rd among top-ranking State Colleges

The Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College ranked 3rd among the top-ranking State Colleges with the highest number of programs granted with Level II accreditation status in 2022 by the Accrediting Agency of Charter Colleges and Universities in the Philippines (AACCUP), Inc. Dr. Gilbert R. Arce, SUC President III, accompanied by other college officials and personnel received the plaque of recognition during the 36th AACCUP Annual National Conference held at Century Park Hotel in Manila City on March 27-29, 2023. The AACCUP is the agency that grants different accreditation status to different State Universities and Colleges in the country after passing the rigorous process of accreditation.
