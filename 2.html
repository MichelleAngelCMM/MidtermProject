ISPSC hosts IRCET 2023

Research is fully embraced by every higher education institution as one of the mandates it has to perform. Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College (ISPSC) and Abra State Institute of Sciences and Technology (ASIST) perform this mandate and commit to imbibing the culture of research for educational transformation and development. This collaborative activity, IRCET 2023, being undertaken by ISPSC and ASIST provides academic institutions and industries the opportunities for intellectual discussion and dissemination of research outputs through presentations. Through this activity, scholars, researchers, and professionals will have the great opportunity to share ideas and experiences that will eventually contribute to educational and technological advancement and development. The 1st International Conference on Education and Technology (IRCET) 2023 which will take place on October 12-13 via Zoom Teleconferencing serves as an academic platform for researchers, professionals, scholars, academicians, and graduate students to present their research findings, the latest educational developments, and emerging technologies. This conference primarily showcases the latest discoveries in the disciplines.
